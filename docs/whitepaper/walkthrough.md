# Walkthrough: KULA Vision & Whitepaper

In this session, we transitioned KULA from a prototype concept into a fully articulated vision for a **Resilient Neighborhood Infrastructure**.

## Accomplishments

### 1. The KULA Manifesto
We established a powerful philosophical core centered on the **"Ode to Collective Existence."** We identified the "Closed-Loop Transaction" as a suffocating narrative for communities and positioned KULA as a tool to solve the **"Problem of the Intermediary."**

### 2. The Staged Roadmap
We defined a clear, three-horizon evolution for the project:
*   **Stage 1:** Establishing the **Social Mycelium** through a trust-based gift economy.
*   **Stage 2:** Launching the **Computational Layer** (Eco-Compute) to fuel a Community Treasury.
*   **Stage 3:** Realizing the **Resilient Infrastructure** through mesh networking and P2P data sovereignty.

### 3. Technical & Social Innovation
We codified several unique concepts:
*   **The KULA Mycelium:** Visualizing indirect social bonds to remove the friction of asking.
*   **The Nightstand Test:** A hardware safety standard for volunteer computing.
*   **The Socioeconomic Mesh:** A state where social trust and physical mesh networks merge for total neighborhood resilience.
