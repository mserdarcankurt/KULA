# 🌱 Funding Opportunities for KULA in Berlin

KULA is a hyper-local mutual aid / gift economy platform — neighborhood-focused, community-driven, with İmece (collaborative work), trust-based sharing, and a warm analog-Berlin aesthetic. Here's a landscape of real funding paths.

---

## 🎯 Tier 1 — Strongest Fit (Apply First)

### 1. Berliner Startup Stipendium (BSS)
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Monthly stipend of **€2,500/founder** for 6–12 months |
| **Who** | Early-stage, research-driven, tech-based startups |
| **Why KULA fits** | Recent batches specifically target **democratic resilience, social stability, and sustainability** — KULA's trust engine and community reciprocity model map perfectly |
| **Current Status** | ⚠️ **Applications for the current batch are closed.** Keep an eye out for future calls, or explore the rolling options below. |
| **How** | Managed through university incubators (Science & Startups, HWR Startup Incubator Berlin). **Book a consultation first** — this is required to get the application link |
| **Links** | [science-startups.berlin](https://science-startups.berlin) · [startup-incubator.berlin](https://startup-incubator.berlin) |

> [!TIP]
> The BSS is the single best entry point. It gives you money to live on, mentorship, and access to university lab infrastructure — without giving up equity.

### 1b. EXIST Business Start-up Grant (EXIST-Gründerstipendium)
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Monthly stipend of **€1,000–€3,000/founder** for 12 months + €30,000 for materials + €10,000 coaching |
| **Who** | Students, graduates, or researchers (up to 5 years post-grad) with a university-affiliated mentor |
| **Why KULA fits** | Knowledge-based service/trust network engine. Social startups qualify if they have an innovative tech core |
| **Current Status** | 🟢 **Open year-round (No deadlines).** Apply through your university's startup service (e.g. Science & Startups) |
| **Links** | [exist.de](https://www.exist.de) |

---

### 2. GründungsBONUS Plus (IBB Business Team)
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Grant covering **up to 50% of eligible costs**, max **€50,000** |
| **Who** | Innovative, sustainable, social startups in Berlin (≤18 months old) |
| **Covers** | Rent, salaries, materials, marketing, prototyping costs |
| **Why KULA fits** | Explicitly includes **social** and **digital** innovation. Community platform for neighborhood sharing is a direct hit |
| **Requirement** | Company must be **registered in Berlin** |
| **Current Status** | 🟢 **Open year-round (Rolling applications).** |
| **Links** | [ibb-business-team.de](https://ibb-business-team.de) |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This requires a registered business entity (GmbH, UG, or Genossenschaft). If you haven't incorporated yet, this is the trigger to do so. A **UG (haftungsbeschränkt)** can be set up for as little as €1 and is the fastest path.

---

### 3. Prototype Fund (BMBF / Open Knowledge Foundation)
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Up to **€47,500** per project over 6 months |
| **Who** | Individuals or small teams building **open-source software** for civic/public interest |
| **Why KULA fits** | Civic tech, community infrastructure, trust systems — this is exactly what they fund. If KULA commits to open-source (even partially), this is a strong match |
| **Rounds** | Applications open in periodic rounds — check site for next deadline |
| **Links** | [prototypefund.de](https://prototypefund.de) |

> [!NOTE]
> The Prototype Fund specifically values projects that **serve the public good through technology**. KULA's mutual aid / gift economy framing, trust engine, and İmece coordination are a compelling narrative here.

## 💡 Framing KULA for Funders

> [!TIP]
> Different funders want different stories. Here are your angles:

| Funder Type | Lead With |
|---|---|
| **Tech grants** (Prototype Fund, Sovereign Tech) | Open-source community infrastructure, trust engine architecture, federated/local-first design |
| **Social impact** (BSS, SOCIA, IBB Impact) | Mutual aid as antidote to isolation, neighborhood resilience, gift economy reducing inequality |
| **Innovation grants** (GründungsBONUS, Pro FIT) | AI-powered İmece orchestration, reputation/trust algorithms, novel platform economics |
| **EU programs** (Horizon, DIGITAL) | Scalable model for European neighborhood solidarity, democratic participation technology |
| **Foundations** (Bürgerstiftung) | Grassroots neighborhood connection, civic engagement, inclusive community building |
